Key Features:
1. AI Chat Widget - Embeddable chat widget that can be integrated into any website

4. Multi-language Support - Support customers in their preferred language
5. Real-time Analytics - Track customer interactions and satisfaction metrics

Technical Support:
For technical issues, please contact our support team at support@makkn.com or use the live chat feature on our website. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Integration Guide:
To integrate the MAKKN chat widget into your website:
1. Sign up for a MAKKN account
2. Create a new project in the dashboard
3. Upload your knowledge base documents
4. Copy the provided embed code
5. Paste the code into your website's HTML before the closing </body> tag
